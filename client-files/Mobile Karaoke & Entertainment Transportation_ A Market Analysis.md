## The Venture-Backed Disruptor: Fetii

A significant development in this space is the emergence of **Fetii** ([fetii.com](https://www.fetii.com)), an on-demand group rideshare app backed by investors like Mark Cuban and Y Combinator. Fetii has raised a $7.35 million seed round and is expanding to multiple cities [5].

**Fetii's Business Model:**

*   **On-Demand Group Travel:** Addresses the logistical challenge of moving groups of people who are too large for a standard Uber or Lyft but too small for a full-sized charter bus.
*   **Technology-Driven:** The app facilitates seamless booking, and a unique QR code-based check-in system allows for easy fare splitting among passengers.
*   **Asset-Light:** Fetii provides its 15-passenger vans to driver-owners, reducing its own capital expenditure.
*   **Targeted Marketing:** Focuses on the Gen Z demographic and key use cases like bachelorette parties, corporate events, and university transportation.
*   **Competitive Pricing:** With rates starting around $100 per hour for a bus, the per-person cost can be significantly lower than traditional party buses or multiple rideshare vehicles.

Fetii's success validates the market demand for a technology-driven, on-demand solution for group transportation and represents a significant competitive threat to both traditional party bus companies and smaller karaoke rideshare services.
